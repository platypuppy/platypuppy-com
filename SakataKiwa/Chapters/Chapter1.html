Chapter 1: "The Investigation"

The girl was biking home from school, taking a shortcut through the forbidden zone due to her club running late. She heard “Gate detected. Coordinates margin of error: 8.03,” looking up to see the gate open above her. She froze, not knowing what to do, as the marmodd dropped from the gate. Instincts kicking in, she drew her sword. She swung it downward onto its head, but the marmodd was unfazed. Realising the situation, she took a step backwards into a defensive stance, but the marmodd took this opening to slice her sword in half. Panic settling in, she dropped the remnants of her sword and fled on her bike.
